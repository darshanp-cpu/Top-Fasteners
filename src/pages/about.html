Our Story

Top Fasteners was founded with a simple mission: to supply South Africa's builders, contractors, and engineers with the highest-quality industrial products at competitive prices.

What started as a small bolt and nut supplier has grown into a comprehensive industrial hardware store, carrying everything from power tools and cutting equipment to plumbing supplies and professional coatings.
